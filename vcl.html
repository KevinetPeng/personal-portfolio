Stepping Up to Own a Project and Lead a Team

My work at Visual Cognition Labs: May 2020 - Dec 2021

Initially, as a Software Developer for the Experiment Framework team, I worked on full-stack feature implementations and a codebase refactor which eventually led to the first successful cloud deployment for the team's experiment framework. This work allowed for asynchronous studies to be run without the need for a researcher to be present, increasing data collection volumes by 10x.
As a result of this work, I was promoted to Lead Software Developer, and given the opportunity to own a new project from the ground up and lead the team responsible for its delivery. Given limited resources, I became the point-of-contact for all stakeholders involved in the project, including lead researchers, lab managers, and the Principal Investigator, and managed a team of Software Developers and UI/UX Designers to deliver a content and documentation management system.

Outcomes

• Led full-stack development efforts, developed high-level designs and architecture, conducted engineering interviews, and reviewed code for all engineers on the team.
• Learned on the fly to manage an interdisciplinary team of Software Developers, UI/UX Designers, and Researchers.
• Wore multiple hats and exceeded my expectations in the role, performing the responsibilities of a Technical Lead, Engineering Manager, and Project Manager.
